## рџ“Ѓ Project Descriptions

### рџ”№ Project 1 вЂ” Exploratory Financial Data Analysis
Focuses on collecting, cleaning, and performing exploratory data analysis (EDA) on real-world financial datasets including equity prices, interest rates, and housing market data.

**Key Topics:** Data acquisition from APIs (Yahoo Finance, FRED), time-series visualization, statistical analysis, and cross-asset comparison.

---

### рџ”№ Project 2 вЂ” Financial Risk & Credit Data Analysis
Analyses credit risk data, bond yields, and fixed income instruments. Covers liquidity assessment and risk profiling across different financial scenarios.

**Key Topics:** Credit rating distributions, mortgage rate analysis, bond yields, illiquid asset pricing, and financial risk modelling.

---

### рџ”№ Project 3 вЂ” Lending & Loan Decision Modelling
Implements quantitative models for loan approval decisions, risk scoring, and lending term generation using Python-based simulations.

**Key Topics:** Credit scoring, loan approval workflows, risk-based interest rate assignment, and OOP-based financial modelling.
